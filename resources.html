FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about encryption and cybersecurity:

What is encryption?
Encryption is the process of converting data into a secret code to protect its confidentiality.
What is cybersecurity?
Cybersecurity refers to the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, and sensitive information from unauthorized access or attack.
What are some common types of cyber attacks?
Some common types of cyber attacks include phishing, malware, ransomware, and denial-of-service (DoS) attacks.
